About

My Aim is to turn Your Vision to Visual Reality

I’m Tomide Erinola, a Full-Stack Python Developer passionate about building functional, scalable web applications that solve real-world problems. I focus on writing clean, maintainable code and developing systems that are easy to understand and extend.

My primary stack includes Python and Flask on the backend, where I design RESTful APIs, handle authentication, and manage application logic. On the frontend, I work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.js to build responsive, interactive user interfaces that provide smooth user experiences.

I have experience working with both SQL and NoSQL databases, including SQLite, PostgreSQL, and MongoDB, allowing me to choose the right data storage solution for different types of applications. I also work with Git and GitHub for version control and enjoy deploying applications to production environments.

Common Questions

Working with me.

I believe in transparency and clear communication. Here are some things clients and employers usually ask about my process.

Still have questions?
What is your typical development process? +

I follow a structured approach: Discovery (understanding your needs), Design (wireframing and UI), Development (building with clean code), and Deployment (testing and going live). I prefer agile iterations to ensure we are always on the same page.

Which technologies do you specialize in? +

My core expertise is in Python (Flask/Django) for backend logic, integrated with clean HTML/CSS/JS for the frontend. I also have experience managing logistics databases and construction quote engines, as seen in my featured projects.

Can you match my specific brand style and colors? +

Yes. I take pride in creating bespoke designs that align perfectly with your existing brand identity. Whether you have a strict style guide or just a logo, I will ensure the UI reflects your brand's personality.

Are you available for freelance or full-time roles? +

I am currently open to both! Whether you need a one-off logistics solution or a dedicated developer to join your engineering team, I'm happy to discuss how I can contribute.

Will the website work on all browsers and devices? +

Absolutely. Every project I develop from logistics systems like JoyXpress to booking apps like Quicklet is built with a "mobile-first" approach. I ensure full compatibility across Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge.

What are your accepted modes of payment? +

I offer flexible payment options to suit international clients, including Bank Transfer, PayPal, Zelle, and Crypto. We can discuss the most convenient method during our initial consultation.
